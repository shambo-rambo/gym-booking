# Booking Rules

## Facilities
- **Gym** — can be booked exclusively (private) or shared (max 2 people)
- **Sauna** — can be booked exclusively (private) or shared (max 2 people at once)

## Hours & Advance Booking
- Open **5:00 AM – 11:00 PM** daily
- Book up to **7 days in advance**
- Sessions are **30 or 60 minutes**

## Per-User Limits (anti-hoarding)
Three rules apply independently per facility (Gym and Sauna tracked separately):

| Rule | Limit |
|---|---|
| Upcoming sessions | Max **3** future sessions per facility |
| Daily time | Max **1 hour** per facility per day (wall-clock, not number of bookings) |
| Consecutive days | Cannot book the **same start time** two days in a row |

If any rule is triggered, the slot shows as **"Limit reached"** — but you can still click it to **join the waitlist**.

## Shared Gym Bookings
- Must select which equipment you'll use (up to 3 items per booking)
- Equipment: Weights Machine, Free Dumbbells, Treadmill, Rowing Machine, Exercise Bike
- If you need more than 3 pieces, book a **Private Gym** session instead

## Exclusive (Private) Bookings
- Blocks the entire facility for the duration — no one else can book that slot
- Blocked if any shared bookings already exist in the overlapping time window

## Queue System
You can join the queue for a slot when:
- The slot is **fully booked** (all equipment taken, sauna full, or exclusive taken), or
- You've hit your personal **booking limit** (waitlist)

**How it works:**
1. When someone cancels, the first person in the queue is notified by email
2. You have **30 minutes** to claim the slot from the notification
3. If you don't claim it, the next person in line is notified
4. Users who are free to book (not limit-blocked) are prioritised ahead of waitlisted users
5. If a slot is not booked 3 hours before the session and the person in queue is allocated the slow.

## Account Registration
- Registering with the **building code** → account is verified immediately
- Registering without the building code → account is **pending manager approval**
- Max **6 residents** per apartment unit
